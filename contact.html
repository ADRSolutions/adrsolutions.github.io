Research & Industry

Herd Diagnostics & AR

Submit a sample

For producers, veterinarians, and researchers submitting population-level fecal samples for parasite diagnostics or anthelmintic resistance monitoring.

ADR Solutions does not accept individual animal samples or perform routine diagnostic testing. Producers and veterinarians seeking routine parasite diagnostics are encouraged to contact a veterinary diagnostic laboratory such as the UGA Tifton Veterinary Diagnostic Laboratory or the UGA Athens Veterinary Diagnostic Laboratory.
