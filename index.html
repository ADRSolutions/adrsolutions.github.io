What we do

ADR Solutions operates at the intersection of research science and real-world animal health offering specialized services in antiparasitic drug screening, population-level diagnostics, and novel platform development.

Drug Screening

Antiparasitic drug screening

In vitro assay panels for pharmaceutical and biotech partners. Quantitative, multi-endpoint results across numerous parasite species and life stages — powered by high-throughput microscopy and custom AI software.

Learn more
Herd Diagnostics & AR

Population-level diagnostics

Fecal analysis and advanced profiling of anthelmintic resistance for livestock, camelids, and companion animals. For producers, veterinarians, and industry clients who need accurate and actionable data — not just a result.

Learn more
Novel Platforms

Novel platform development

Phenotypic and genetic platforms for target screening and discovery. When validated screening methods or capacity doesn't exist for a target, we build it — with milestone-based delivery for pharma partners.
