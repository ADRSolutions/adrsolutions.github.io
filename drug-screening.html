How it works

A modern screening pipeline, end to end.

From compound logistics to image acquisition to AI-derived phenotypes — every step is designed for throughput, reproducibility, and the kind of endpoints discovery teams actually use.

01 · Acquire

HT microscopy

Automated imaging across plate formats and life stages. Multi-well captures with consistent conditions, calibrated optics, and full chain-of-custody on samples and compounds.

02 · Analyze

Custom AI software

Computer-vision models trained on parasite and stage-specific phenotypes, including motility, viability, and development. Tuned per species for sensitivity and specificity.

03 · Deliver

Quantitative endpoints

Multi-endpoint readouts of drug action and kinetics to define dose–response (EC50/IC50) and hit calls. Complete QC visuals and exportable raw data. Reports built for discovery teams.
