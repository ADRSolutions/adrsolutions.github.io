Why it matters

Anthelmintic resistance is widespread — and growing.

Resistance to the major anthelmintic drug classes is documented worldwide, and blanket treatment is losing efficacy. Without population-level data, there's no reliable way to know which drugs still work in your herd. ADR Solutions provides the quantitative diagnostics to find out.

  • Semi-quantitative egg counts
  • Species-level larval identification to guide treatment
  • Resistance profiling to identify which drugs will work best
  • Herd-level pipelines and data
What we offer

Three areas of diagnostic focus.

All work is herd or population-level only. We do not accept individual animal diagnostic cases. Contact us to discuss what testing is appropriate for your situation.

Fecal egg monitoring

Semi-quantitative assessment of parasite egg burden across your herd or population. The baseline data needed for targeted selective treatment programs and resistance surveillance.

Resistance surveillance

Field-based resistance testing to evaluate the efficacy of anthelmintic treatments in your herd. Identifies resistance before it becomes a crisis and informs drug selection.

Customized plans

Evidence-based parasite management programs tailored to your herd, species, and resistance profile — going beyond a single test result to support long-term sustainable parasite control.
